Sensor Logger

A few years ago, I visited my aunt who resides in Central Kenya, situated in a tea estate where daily weather data collection is crucial. Despite her office responsibilities, she had to manually collect data at 9 a.m. and 3 p.m. At that time, I was still in high school, and while I lacked experience, I couldn't help but consider a solution when I saw an IoT project listed on Holberton's portfolio list. This is when the idea for the sensor data logger began to take shape. The project has taken 3 weeks to build and improvements will be taken to ensure everything runs smoothely
